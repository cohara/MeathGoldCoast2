BOYNE STANDUP PADDLE BOARDING (SUP) RACE

Stand up Paddling is one of the fastest growing sports in the world today.

It is a sport that promotes balance while strengthening your core muscles and enhancing all over body fitness and well-being.

The sport requires minimal equipment : simply a long board, extendable paddle and flotation jacket, which makes it attractive to people of all ages and fitness levels and abilities.

The inaugural Boyne SUP Race will take place on Saturday 14th June @ 14:15.

If you are interested in competing in the race please contact Paul Byrne at info@irishsup.com
