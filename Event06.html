EXTREME SPORTS

The organisers of The Irish Maritime Festival 2014 will be welcoming avid fans of water-sports to the area for a fun packed schedule of entertainment and excitement

Showstoppers will include, Water-skiing, Jet-Skiing, Wakeboarding and Barefooting by a team of world-class athletes wowing the crowds with inverts, tricks & jumps and the special appearance of an expert demonstrating the FLYBOARD, a jet-powered board that sends the operator soaring into the air.
