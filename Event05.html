Ruth - A 100ft Baltic Trader - Sweden

Bessie Ellen - 110 Ft Trading Ketch - Plymouth, Devon , England

Soteria - 100ft Gaff Rig Schooner - Denmark

Keewaydin - 90ft BrixhamTrawler - North Sea, England/Denmark.

Vilma - 90ft Topsail Schooner, Wales

Hebble Sand - Ireland/UK
