BOYNE BOAT RACE

Inver Colpa Coastal Rowing Club Drogheda is hosting the Boyne Boat Race from Clogherhead beach to Drogheda town on Sunday 15th June.

Clubs from all over the Island of Ireland have been invited to compete in this festival race.

For more information please visit www.invercolpa.ie


Day :Sunday 15th June 2014

Start : Clogherhead Beach

Finish : Drogheda Port

Time: 11:00 - 13:15 Sunday 15th June 2014
