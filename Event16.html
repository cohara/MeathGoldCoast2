JOHN HOLLAND MEMORIAL STATUTE (1840 – 1914)

John Philip Holland is widely regarded as the father of the modern submarine. He was an Irish Engineer who developed the first submarine to be formally commissioned by the US and first Royal Navy submarine the Holland 1. He lived in Drogheda, in what is now known as Scholars Hotel and Restaurant. The owner of Scholars, Martin McGowan, will unveil a memorial to Mr. Holland’s world renowned legacy during the festival week-end.

For more information please visit www.scholarshotel.com
