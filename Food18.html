Boynegrove farm

Since 1969 MacNeice Fruit Ltd. Has been growing and processing high quality Bramely apples in county Armagh , Northern Ireland. We are a family business , with our own orchard holding, producing a range of the finest fruit products under the Ardress brand. These include canned sliced and diced Bramley apple, apple sauce , canned rhubarb, ready to use pie fillings and sweet mince.Our processing facility has achieved certification tot he BRC Global Food Standard , reagarded as the benchmark for best practice in the food industry.
